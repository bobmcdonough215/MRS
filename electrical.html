Master Repair Services

Master Repair Services is a family owned and operated business with a proven track record of providing Philadelphia with exceptional, reliable, and friendly home repair and renovations. Our skilled and detail-oriented craftsmen are licensed general contractors ready to take on any job. Whether it's fixing a leaky sink or a new roof installation, Master Repair Services has you covered from the ground up.


Over the last 20 years, Master Repair Services has evolved from a small handyman services company into a fully licensed general contracting company working with commercial and residential clients. Our licensed and insured general contractors are happy to help your family or business when it comes to Home Repair, Home Renovations, Electrical Services, Plumbing, Painting, Roofing, and Mold Inspections.
